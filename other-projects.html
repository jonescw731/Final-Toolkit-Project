Neil Nathan is a folk americana artist who consistently works with local Philly musicians. He features Rosie McNamara-Jones on several tracks. She worked closely with him to produce this single, "Love You 'Til the End," which came out July 2024 and features Rosie on the violin.

The Hill recruited a range of Philly musicians to help put together their debut album, featuring Rosie on violin. The album was released in April 2024.
