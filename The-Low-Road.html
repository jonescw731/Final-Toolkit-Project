The Low Road

Rosie McNamara-Jones

Mark Schrieber

Mike Brenner

Palmer Yale

Allan Hewitt

The Low Road was a Philadelphia based band that played countless shows across the city, the surrounding tri-state area, and other parts of the country. They released four albums between 1990 and 1997, touring with bands such as the Bare Naked Ladies. The band is comprised of five members: Mike Brenner on guitar and lead vocals, Mark Schreiber on drums and vocals, Rosie McNamara-Jones on violin and vocals, Palmer Yale on harmonica and vocals, and Allan Hewitt on upright bass.
